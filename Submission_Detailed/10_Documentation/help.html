Frequently Asked Questions & Guide
How does the Route Optimizer work? ▼
The Smart Map uses Dijkstra's algorithm to calculate the shortest path between rides, considering real-time wait times to minimize your walking and waiting duration.
What do the map colors mean? ▼
The Smart Map shows real-time locations of all rides and facilities. Click on any pin to see details like wait times and status.
How accurate is the Crowd Forecast? ▼
Our AI model uses historical data and weather patterns to predict crowd levels with over 85% accuracy.
Can I book tickets here? ▼
Currently, this portal is for planning and monitoring. Ticket booking is available on our main website.
